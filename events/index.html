Classes and Events

There’s almost always something happening at PFG! We offer glassblowing classes at the studio year-round, and ornament sessions for small groups around the holidays. In the spring and summer, Jim takes the glass on the road to regional art festivals (mostly in Illinois). We also occasionally host special events or group demonstrations by appointment.

Demonstrations, Ornament Sessions, and Classes

Group Tours and Demonstrations (September through May)

We’re happy to offer group tours of our gallery and glassblowing space, including live demonstrations. We’ll teach your group about the equipment, tools, and skills needed to perform this age-old art. Contact us to schedule a tour or to arrange for a special demonstration.

Ornament Sessions (October through April)

  • Ages 5+
  • $20 per ornament (minimum of 8)
  • Approximately 1-1.5 hours for 8 ornaments

Guaranteed fun for all ages! You pick the colors and provide the air, and we do the rest. Great for individuals, families, scout, church, and school groups who want to experience glassblowing. Make them for yourself or to give as gifts. We also carry a large assortment of ornament holders so you can display and enjoy your keepsake ornaments throughout the year.

Please call in advance to schedule an appointment — we’ll make every effort to arrange a session time to fit your needs. A deposit or credit card number is required to reserve a time. (Note: the ornaments will need to cool down and will be ready to pick up in 48 hours. Well worth the wait!)

Intro to Hot Glass

  • Ages 18+
  • Saturdays from 2:00–5:00pm or Thursdays from 6:00–9:00pm
  • Maximum 4 students per session
  • $75

Learn the basic skills of handling hot glass! You’ll participate in the exciting process of creating a color swirl glass paperweight. No previous glassblowing experience required.

Registration must be made in advance, and paid in full at the time of the application. Students can register in two ways:

  1. In person at Prairie Fire Glass, 217 W. Washington, Monticello, IL.
  2. By phone (217-762-3332) during business hours, using Visa or Mastercard.

Students will be admitted on a first-come, first-served basis. Classes fill up quickly, and waiting lists are maintained for all filled classes. Applicants will be notified, should space become available.

If it’s necessary for Prairie Fire Glass to cancel a class for any reason, students will be notified and will be offered the option of another class or a refund.
