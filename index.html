Prairie Fire Glass is a glassworking studio and gallery, owned and operated by artist Jim Downey in Monticello, Illinois.

Jim creates beautiful and functional glass works like vases, bowls, jewelry, and ornaments. Each piece is one of a kind and made by hand! We have a wide range of sizes and styles, but if you can’t find what you’re looking for, we take custom orders too. Here are a few recent works:

When you visit the gallery, you can often watch live glassblowing demos in the back of the building, while you browse the finished works in the front. Along with our handmade glass, we stock mixed-media work from local artists. You’ll find everything from greeting cards to paintings, sculpture, and photography.

Prairie Fire Glass is a family-run small business, and we’re proud to be one of the eclectic restaurants and shops in downtown Monticello, Illinois. Monticello’s a hidden gem that’s a great place to visit. It’s just a 30-minute drive from Champaign! Come on by and spend the morning with us, eat a tasty lunch across the street at the Brown Bag, and go for an afternoon walk outside at historic Allerton Park.

If you can’t make it to Monticello, no problem. You can order direct and we’ll ship your items in just a few days. In the warmer months, Jim also tours the Midwest at regional art fairs. Check the schedule to see if we’re coming to a town near you.
