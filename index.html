Prairie Fire Glass is a professional glassworking studio, owned and operated by artist Jim Downey in Monticello, Illinois.

Our studio has a unique setup, with a gallery in the front of the building and our live workshop in the back. When you visit in person, you can watch live glassblowing demos while browsing our many finished works.

We also stock mixed-media work from local artists — you’ll find everything from greeting cards to paintings, sculpture, and photography.

Prairie Fire Glass is a family-run small business, and we’re proud to be one of the eclectic restaurants and shops in downtown Monticello. Monticello’s a hidden gem that’s a great place to visit, but if you can’t make it in person, no problem! You can order direct instead and we’ll ship your items in just a few days.

In the warmer months, Jim takes his glass on the road to several regional Midwest art fairs. Check the schedule to see if we’re coming to a town near you.
