Welcome to Prairie Fire Glass! We’re a professional glassworking studio located in Monticello, Illinois.

Owned and operated by glass artist Jim Downey, Prairie Fire Glass features beautiful and functional artworks like vases, bowls, jewelry, and ornaments. Each piece is one of a kind, handmade on site. Here are a few recent works:

See more in our online shop →

Our unique building has a gallery in the front and a working studio in the back, so when you visit in person, you can watch live glassblowing demos while browsing the extensive variety of finished pieces.

We also stock mixed-media work from local artists — you’ll find everything from greeting cards to paintings, sculpture, and photography.

Prairie Fire Glass is a family-run small business, and we’re proud to be one of the eclectic restaurants and shops in downtown Monticello. Monticello’s a hidden gem that’s a great place to visit, but if you can’t make it in person, no problem! You can order direct and we’ll ship your items in just a few days.

In the warmer months, Jim takes the glass on the road to several Midwest art festivals. Check the schedule to see if he’s coming to a town near you.
