Welcome to Prairie Fire Glass! We’re a professional glassworking studio and gallery in Monticello, Illinois.

Our unique retail store has a gallery in the front and a working studio in the back, so when you visit in person, you can watch live glassblowing demos while browsing the extensive variety of finished pieces.

Along with the glass, we stock mixed-media work from local artists — you’ll find everything from greeting cards to paintings, sculpture, and photography.

Prairie Fire Glass is a family-run small business, and we’re proud to be one of the eclectic restaurants and shops in downtown Monticello. Monticello’s a hidden gem that’s a great place to visit, but if you can’t make it in person, no problem! You can order direct and we’ll ship your items in just a few days.
